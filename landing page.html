About Community Disaster Response

In times of crisis, coordination can save lives. Many disaster-prone communities face challenges due to delayed communication and lack of reliable updates.

This platform bridges that gap with real-time maps, crowdsourced alerts, and volunteer coordination tools.

Our mission: empower communities with technology so no one is left behind in a disaster.
