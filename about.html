ℹ️ About This App

Our Community Disaster Response App helps you stay safe and connected during emergencies.

🎯 Key Features:

  • Real-time disaster alerts and updates
  • Interactive safe zone and evacuation maps
  • One-touch SOS alerts to emergency contacts
  • Community volunteer coordination
  • Personal emergency planning tools
  • Offline access to critical information

🤝 How It Works:

  • Stay Informed: Get instant notifications about local emergencies
  • Get Help: Send location-based SOS alerts when in danger
  • Help Others: Join volunteer efforts in your community
  • Be Prepared: Access emergency plans and resources

📱 Supported Platforms:

  • Web browsers (Chrome, Firefox, Safari, Edge)
  • iOS and Android mobile apps
  • Works offline for critical features

💡 Tip:

Create an account to unlock all features including SOS alerts and volunteer coordination.
